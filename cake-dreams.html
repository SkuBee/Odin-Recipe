Don’t wait until it’s too late. Eat that slice of cake! What? Too late!

Pure loveliness, and low cal!

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1 1/2 c white flour
  • 1/2 tsp minced or grated ginger
  • 3 c of sugar
  • 1 tsp dried unicorn horn
  • 1 full day of happiness

Frosting

  • 2 c Vegan butter
  • 5 c confectionary sugar
  • a tiny sprinkle of envy(not too much, otherwise it will be bitter)

Let's get cooking!

Make the cake

  1. Pre heat oven to 333 degrees F
  2. Mix all the ingredients together and whip until frothy
  3. Add to a bundt cake pan and cook for 38 minutes
  4. Remove when cooked, set aside

Make the frosting

  1. In a skillet, heat the butter over medium-high heat. Add the sugar and cook until melted
  2. Reduce the heat to low and add the envy
  3. Let cool for 34 hours
  4. Frost cake and leave it in the alley
  5. Have a seat and enjoy a cigarette. You've earned it!
